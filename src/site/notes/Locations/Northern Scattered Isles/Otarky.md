Island in the [[Locations/Northern Scattered Isles/Northern Scattered Isles\|Northern Scattered Isles]], where giant mushrooms grow. The town of Otarkys in the South extracts fibres from the mushrooms. Otarkyn in the North specialises in shroomwood.
